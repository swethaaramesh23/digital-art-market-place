Frequently Asked Questions

What is an NFT?

NFT stands for Non-Fungible Token. It is a unique digital asset verified using blockchain technology, giving buyers proof of ownership and authenticity.

How do I buy an NFT on Stackly?

Simply connect your crypto wallet, browse the marketplace, and click 'Place a bid' or 'Buy Now' on any artwork you like.

What are the gas fees?

Gas fees vary depending on the blockchain you choose. Stackly supports Ethereum, Polygon, and Solana to give you high speed and low cost options.
