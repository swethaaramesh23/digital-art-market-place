Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an NFT on Stackly?

Sign up as a Creator, navigate to your Artist Dashboard, and click the 'Mint New NFT' button. You can upload various file formats up to 100MB.

What are the platform fees?

Stackly charges a flat 2% fee on all primary and secondary sales, which is one of the lowest in the industry. Gas fees are calculated based on network congestion.

How do I report a stolen artwork?

Please use the contact form above and select "Copyright Infringement" as the subject. Provide links to the original artwork and proof of ownership, and our moderation team will review it within 24 hours.

Can I change my wallet address?

Currently, accounts are tied directly to the wallet they were created with. In our upcoming V2.0 update, you will be able to link multiple wallet addresses to a single user profile.
